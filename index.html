Instruction

• All mines in superposition - are at several tiles
• Probability is equal 1 / mine-linked tile count
• Tiles show sum of probabilities around
• After collapse all mine go to random linked tile
• To safety collapse tile use quantum flag and "Collapse" button
• You will win, when all tiles are opened and all mines was collapsed and marked classic flags
• Quantum Mines - percent of mines from total tiles count
• Uncertainty Factor - percent of tiles that will be distributed to mines from total tiles count
